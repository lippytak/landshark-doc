• March 1 client applies for CalFresh

    During the application process, the eligibility worker collects the client's cell phone number and asks:

    Would you like to receive text message notifications for your CalFresh quarterly report at this phone number?

  • March 4 client gets welcome message

    If the client says yes, they receive a welcome message a few days later.

    Hello! We will send you text message reminders for your CalFresh quarterly report (QR-7). If you have questions, call (555) 555-5555. Text STOP to unsubscribe.

  • May 25 one week before quarterly report is due

    One week before the quarterly report is due, they are sent a text message reminder.

    Your CalFresh quarterly report (QR-7) is due on Thursday, December 21st. If you have questions, call (555) 555-5555.
