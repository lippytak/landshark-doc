• Reminder variants

    The fact that someone has enrolled in CalFresh is confidential information. In addition to asking clients to explicitly opt-in to receiving reminders, we want to be mindful not to divulge excess information in the reminder messages themselves.

  • Reminder - basic

    This is the basic reminder, including the program, report description, due date, and contact number for questions.

    Your CalFresh (Food Stamps) quarterly report (QR-7) is due on Thursday, December 21st. If you have questions, call (555) 555-5555.

  • Reminder - no program name

    This variant omits specific references to CalFresh.

    Your quarterly report (QR-7) is due on Thursday, December 21st. If you have questions, call (555) 555-5555.

  • Reminder - no program name, no date

    This variant omits specific references to CalFresh and the deadline date.

    You have a report due soon. If you have questions, call (555) 555-5555.

  • Reminder - no program, no date, no contact number

    This variant omits specific references to CalFresh, the deadline date, and the CalFresh contact number.

    You have a report due soon. If you have questions, please call your worker.
